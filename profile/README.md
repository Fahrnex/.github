# Welcome to Fahrnex 👋

### Maintain. Manage. Move Forward.

Fahrnex is a modern vehicle maintenance and fleet management platform that helps drivers and businesses stay organized, compliant, and always one step ahead. By bringing your vehicles, maintenance schedules, TÜV deadlines, and operating expenses into a single intuitive interface, we empower smarter fleet operations.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

*   📅 **Maintenance Reminders & TÜV Deadlines** – Never miss a critical vehicle inspection or service date again.
*   💰 **Expense & Fuel Tracking** – Keep a close eye on your fleet's total cost of ownership and reduce unnecessary expenses.
*   📂 **Document Management** – Store all vehicle registration documents, insurance papers, and service histories in one place.
*   🚛 **Fleet Operations Hub** – Monitor real-time status and operational readiness for your entire fleet.
